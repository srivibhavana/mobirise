ABOUT US


Founded in 2003, SVE Engineering Equipments. is the leader in the design and manufacture of conveying equipment,bulk material storage and air pollution control equipments. We are dedicated in providing the highest quality equipment and service to our customers.

SVE products are supplied to many diverse industries and to a large customer base. Our primary customers are end users, engineering firms, poultry feed firms, sugar plant, cement plants fertilizer industries, tyre industries, paint industries, ceramic industries and original equipment manufacturers (OEMs).

We are located in Ambattur Industrial Estate, Chennai

SVE is an ISO 9001-2008 certified manufacturer. We are committed to customer satisfaction and continuous improvement of our products and services.

SCREW CONVEYORS

Screw conveyors are used in thousands of industrial applications every day for efficiently conveying a variety of bulk materials. A screw conveyor’s main function is to transfer bulk materials from one process to another. Screw conveyors are very cost-effective and require minimal maintenance to operate.

DUST COLLECTORS

Our optimum designed Dust collectors capture the dust particle from the air-stream by forcing the airflow through the filter bags. The filter bags are selected very care fully for optimum air to cloth ration for efficient filtering.. Our dust collectors can be used in varieties of applications from ambient temperature to high temperature and also for corrosive and hazardous applications.

WET SCRUBBERS

Our Wet scrubbers are very effective air pollution control devices for removing particles and/or gases from industrial exhaust streams. Our wet scrubber operates by introducing the dirty gas stream with a scrubbing liquid – typically water. Particulate or gases are collected in the scrubbing liquid. Wet scrubbers are generally the most appropriate air pollution control device for collecting both particulate and gas in a single system.We offer a variety of wet scrubber systems specifically designed for your process applications

CYCLONE SEPERATOR

 Our cyclone provides solutions to the problem of collecting materials both for exhuasting dust and conveying bark and chips. The centrifugal action causes the air and material to move around the inside wall of the cyclone, from where the material drops by force of gravity to the bottom, leaving the clean air to escape through the top. Our design allows low resistance to flow,adequate capacity and reliabilty.Our light duty cyclone is very effective for non-abrasive,granular/fibrous dusts and the heavier will be used for chips or other material in pieces
